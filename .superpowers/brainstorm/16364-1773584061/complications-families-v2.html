Which complication slots should Daily Ascent support?

Each is a different shape/size slot on the watch face. Select the ones you want.

Watch face (Infograph)
10:09
MON 16 MAR
D — 4 exercises today
DUE 2/4
2 remaining
A — Circular (top-left)
B — Corner (top-right)
C — Rectangular (bottom)
D — Inline (top text)
A

Circular

Small circle. Shows "2/4" or "REST". Most common slot — works on almost every watch face.

B

Corner

Arc gauge in the corner. Only on Infograph-style faces. A bit niche.

C

Rectangular

Wider slot with more room. Shows remaining count + coloured dots. Available on Modular and others.

D

Inline

Single line of text at the top. Very simple — just "4 exercises today" or "Rest day".

Recommended: A + C (Circular + Rectangular) — covers the most popular watch faces.
