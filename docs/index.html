How it works

Three steps. Real progression.

Every exercise follows the same clear loop. No randomised workouts, no subscription walls.

1

Enrol

Pick from nine exercise groups. New to pull-ups or dips? Start with the Prepare tier. Ready for more? Jump straight to Level 1.

2

Train

Follow your prescribed sets and reps each training day. Rest days are calculated for you based on when you last trained.

3

Level up

Finish the programme and take a max-rep test. Hit the target? You unlock the next level. Clear, earned progression.

Features

Everything you need to get stronger.

▲ Master your bodyweight

Push-Ups, Squats, Pull-Ups, Dead Bugs, Hip Hinge, Spinal Extension, Plank, Rows, and Dips. Not ready for Level 1? Pull-Ups, Push-Ups, and Dips include a Prepare tier with easier variations to build you up. From complete beginner to advanced, every training day is mapped out.

📅 Smart scheduling

Rest days adjust automatically based on when you actually trained. Miss a session? Your schedule shifts. No lost progress.

📈 Earned progression

Each level ends with a max-rep test. Pass it and move up. Long-press any level to preview the full day-by-day plan before you start.

⏱ Train from your wrist

Apple Watch counts reps, times rest periods, and logs sessions. Leave your phone in your pocket.

🏆 Achievements

Earn badges for milestones, streaks, consistency, and more as you train.

🔒 Private by design

Everything runs on your device. Optional community rankings use fully anonymous data you can delete with one tap.
