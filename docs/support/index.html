Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use Daily Ascent?

No, Daily Ascent does not require you to create an account. All your workout data and history are stored securely and locally on your device.

How does the Apple Watch app work?

Just open the Daily Ascent app on your Apple Watch. It automatically syncs with the active schedule on your iPhone, allowing you to log sets, count reps, and time your rest periods without needing to pick up your phone.

I missed a workout day. Is my schedule ruined?

Not at all! Daily Ascent uses Smart Scheduling. If you miss a session, your rest days and upcoming workouts simply shift forward automatically based on when you actually train. You never lose your progress.
