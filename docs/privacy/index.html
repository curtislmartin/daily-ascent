Privacy Policy

Inch Bodyweight Training — Last updated March 2026

Overview

Inch is a bodyweight training app for iPhone and Apple Watch. It helps you track workouts across six exercises — Push-Ups, Squats, Sit-Ups, Pull-Ups, Glute Bridges, and Dead Bugs — and progress through structured training levels.

Inch does not require an account, does not collect your name or email, and does not use advertising or third-party analytics. Most data never leaves your device.

Data We Collect

The table below summarises everything Inch stores or transmits:

  • Workout history — stored locally on your device using SwiftData. Never uploaded to any server.
  • HealthKit data — calories burned and active minutes, read and written to the Apple Health app on your device. Stays on your device and your personal iCloud account. Inch does not receive or store this data on any external server.
  • Motion sensor data (optional) — accelerometer and gyroscope readings captured during workouts, only if you explicitly opt in. See the section below.

Inch collects no location data, no contacts, no camera or microphone access, no browsing history, and no advertising identifiers.

HealthKit

If you grant permission, Inch reads and writes workout data (active calories, exercise minutes) to Apple Health. This data is governed by Apple's HealthKit policies and stays within the Apple ecosystem on your device and your private iCloud account.

Inch never sends HealthKit data to any external server. You can revoke Health access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Inch.

Motion Data (Optional)

Inch includes an optional feature to help improve automatic rep counting in future versions. If you enable "Share motion data anonymously" in Settings, Inch will record accelerometer and gyroscope data from your iPhone and Apple Watch during workouts and upload it to a secure server.

This upload is:

  • Truly anonymous — uploaded with no identifier of any kind. No user ID, device ID, or random token is included. Records cannot be traced back to any individual.
  • Used only for training ML models for rep counting. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any other purpose.
  • Opt-in only — the toggle is off by default. You can disable it at any time in the app's Settings screen.

If you do not enable this toggle, no motion data is uploaded. Ever.

Data Retention

Local data (workout history, settings) is retained on your device until you delete the app.

Uploaded motion data, if you opted in, is retained on our server for as long as it is useful for improving rep counting models, and deleted thereafter. Uploaded records contain no identifier of any kind — not a user ID, device ID, or random token — so it is not possible to associate them with any individual or to selectively delete a specific person's data. If you have concerns, please contact us before enabling the toggle.

Children

Inch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
